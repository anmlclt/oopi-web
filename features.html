Powerful Features

Discover everything that makes Oopi the ultimate photo transformation app

100+ Art Styles

Explore an ever-growing collection of artistic styles ranging from classical masters like Van Gogh and Monet to modern digital art, anime, cyberpunk, watercolor, oil painting, sketch, pop art, and countless more. Each style is carefully crafted to deliver stunning results.

Lightning Fast

Our optimized AI processes your photos in seconds, not minutes. Transform multiple photos quickly and efficiently. No more waiting around - instant gratification with every transformation.

AI-Powered Intelligence

State-of-the-art machine learning models understand the content of your photos and apply styles with incredible precision. Faces, landscapes, objects - everything is handled with intelligence and care.

High Resolution

Export your transformed photos in full high resolution, perfect for printing large formats, professional use, or sharing on social media. No compression, no quality loss - just pure art.

Unlimited Transforms

No limits, no restrictions. Transform the same photo with different styles as many times as you want. Experiment freely and discover your perfect artistic expression.

Intuitive Interface

Beautiful, clean design that anyone can use. No technical knowledge required. Pick a photo, choose a style, tap transform - it's that simple. Beauty meets functionality.

Seamless iOS Integration

Built specifically for iOS with native performance and design. Integrates perfectly with your Photos app, iCloud, and the entire Apple ecosystem. Feels right at home on your iPhone.

Custom Intensity

Adjust the intensity of each style to get exactly the look you want. From subtle artistic touches to bold transformations - you're in complete control of your creative vision.

Batch Processing

Transform multiple photos at once with batch processing. Perfect for creating consistent looks across photo series or quickly processing your entire vacation album.

Favorites & History

Save your favorite styles for quick access and view your transformation history. Never lose track of that perfect style combination you discovered.

Privacy First

Your photos are processed securely and never stored on our servers. Complete privacy and peace of mind. What's yours stays yours - always.

Regular Updates

New styles, features, and improvements added regularly. We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible with AI photo transformation.
