Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

At Oopi, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our photo transformation application.

Information We Collect

When you use Oopi, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Photos: The photos you choose to transform using our app. These photos are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed, styles used, and app performance data.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Analytics: Anonymous analytics data to help us improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our photo transformation services
  • To improve and optimize the app experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and app performance
  • To provide customer support
  • To develop new features and styles
  • To comply with legal obligations

Photo Processing and Storage

Your privacy is paramount. All photo transformations are processed on your device using on-device AI technology. Your original photos and transformed images are not uploaded to our servers or stored in our cloud unless you explicitly choose to use cloud backup or sharing features.

When you use our services, photos are temporarily cached on your device for processing purposes and are automatically deleted after the transformation is complete.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app, such as:

  • Analytics services (anonymous usage data only)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (for app functionality)
  • Customer support tools

These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics and data collection in the app settings
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format

Children's Privacy

Oopi is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using Oopi, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
